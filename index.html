Our Products

What We Trade & Manufacture

Trader of GAIL, BCPL, HMEL, Haldia, OPAL, MRPL, HPCL, Reliance & Nayara

Sree Mantralaya Petrochem is a leading trader of all kinds of plastic raw materials & imported granules for domestic and imported polymers — GAIL, BCPL, HMEL, Haldia, OPAL, MRPL, HPCL, Reliance & Nayara for Polypropylene and Polyethylene in Andhra Pradesh & Telangana. We also trade Metallocene, PVC, PS, PET, EVA & Polystyrene.

Polypropylene (PP)

Homopolymer
  • Textile wraps, garment bags, snack food packing
  • Extrusion coating, fibre & filament
  • Multifilament yarn, woven sacks, and non-woven fabric
  • Rigid packing, thin wall containers, multicavity moulding
  • Injection moulding & compounding applications

Impact Co-Polymer

Polypropylene
  • Furniture, caps, and closures
  • Sheets, blow moulded containers
  • House wares, general products moulding
  • Paint pans, luggage, industrial components
  • Appliances, automotive compounding
  • Batteries, automotive applications

Polyethylene (PE)

HDPE · LDPE · LLDPE
  • Woven sacks for fertilizers, food grains
  • Blown film & co-extrusion for lamitube
  • Blow moulding containers up to 100 litres
  • Drip lateral application
  • Overhead & loft tanks, bins
  • Extrusion coating with/without slip
  • Heavy duty bags, greenhouse films

Specialty Polymers

Trading · Distribution · Solutions
  • Metallocene — advanced performance resins
  • PVC — pipes, profiles, cables
  • Polystyrene (PS) — packaging, disposables
  • PET — bottles, containers, films
  • EVA — footwear, solar encapsulants
  • Imported granules & masterbatches
  • Additives, pigments & custom compounding
