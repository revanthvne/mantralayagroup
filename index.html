What We Trade & Manufacture

Dealer of GAIL, BCPL, HMEL, Reliance, Haldia, OPAL & MRPL

Sree Mantralaya Petrochem is a Dealer of GAIL, BCPL, HMEL, Reliance, Haldia, OPAL & MRPL for Polypropylene and Polyethylene — ranked #2 in Andhra Pradesh & Telangana. We also trade Metallocene, PVC, PS, PET, and EVA.

Polypropylene (PP)

Homopolymer · Random Co-Polymer · Impact Co-Polymer
  • Textile wraps, garments bags, snack food packing (H100EY)
  • Extrusion coating (H350ECF), fibre & filament
  • Multifilament yarn (H200PG), non-woven fabric (H350FG)
  • Rigid packing, furniture, thin wall containers, multicavity moulding
  • Compounding grades (H110MA, AM120N, H200MA, AM650M)
  • High clarity bottles, containers, sheets (SR20NC)
  • Impact: automotive, batteries (C060MT), appliances (B400MG, B650MG)

Polyethylene (PE)

HDPE · LDPE · LLDPE
  • Woven sacks for fertilizers, food grains (ES30009)
  • Blown film & co-extrusion for lamitube (F46003, S46001)
  • Blow moulding containers up to 100 litres (S2GB002)
  • Drip lateral application (CR 11 — 3618010)
  • Overhead & loft tanks, bins (LL360A045, LL360A028)
  • Extrusion coating with/without slip grades
  • Heavy duty bags, greenhouse films (23FA002, 22FA003)

Specialty Polymers

Trading · Distribution · Solutions
  • Metallocene — advanced performance resins
  • PVC — pipes, profiles, cables
  • PS — packaging, disposables
  • PET — bottles, containers, films
  • EVA — footwear, solar encapsulants
  • Masterbatches & base resins (M42500, M53500, M83500)
  • Additives, pigments & custom compounding
